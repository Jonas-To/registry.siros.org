Badge Styles

Shields.io supports multiple badge styles. Change style=flat to one of:

  • flat - Default flat style
  • flat-square - Flat with square corners
  • for-the-badge - Larger badge for prominent display
  • plastic - 3D plastic look
  • social - Social media style
